Come, Uncover our Story

Chai Govindam is a brand that brings to you the authenticity of a perfect desi Chai. Chai Govindam came into existence in the year 2017 by Mr. Namit Goyal when he decided to brew an underrated idea of starting a Chai business. The main aim behind Namit's ideology of Chai Govindam was a space that connects people to their heritage and habitual comfort by letting them enjoy a daily cup of chai without compromising on hygiene

Our Vision

Chai Govindam's desire is to watch people share happiness with a cup of Chai as an excuse to share good times with great minds. We wish to see ourselves as revolutionary change makers in the market of Chai business.

Our Mision

Chai Govindam delivers an exceptionally unique platform that provides the finest quality of healthy desi Chai with utmost satisfaction and pleasure to the customers. We wish to bring forth an opportunity for struggling Indian Chaiwalas by converting the old unorganized sector of Chai into a retail chai-chain.

Our Promise

Chai Govindam is committed to deliver a Flavourful, hygienic, unforgettable experience every time you visit. Our promise? To keep raising the quality and taste, ensuring that each cup is crafted with care and love. Chai Govindam is here to make your chai experience better than ever, now and always.
